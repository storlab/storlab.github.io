StorLab Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 23, 2020

By installing and launching StorLab mobile applications or games, you agree and consent to all terms and conditions specified in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used, and disclosed by StorLab when you access or use StorLab websites and mobile applications.  This policy applies to StorLab websites, games, applications, and other technologies.

Advertising is a core part of StorLab business. Many of our free mobile games, mobile applications and technologies are supported by the ads we and others display. By installing and launching our games you understand and consent to our use of data as defined in our Policy.

Our Privacy Pledge

Our commitment is to put users first. We strive to be transparent about how we collect and use information from our users, to keep the information we collect secure, and to provide users meaningful choices. StorLab has intentionally avoided collecting data that identifies specific individuals (such as name, email address, physical address, GPS or phone number) to the extent possible. 

Collection of Information

We collect the following types of information when you interact with our Services:

  • Device Identifiers. When you interact with our Services, we do not require you to provide information that identifies you personally.  Instead, we collect and identify your device primarily using your mobile device advertising identifier, e.g. Apple’s iOS Identifier for Advertising (IDFA) or Google’s Android Advertising ID, as well as other identifiers.
  • User Names.If you would like to participate in our leaderboards, keep track of your game progress, we may ask you to provide a username. To help protect your privacy, please do not choose a username which could be used to personally identify you.
  • Other Information You Provide to Us. We collect the information that you provide to us, for example when you communicate with others or post, use messenger or chat services, upload or store content, contact our Technical Support team, or provide billing information.
  • Log Information.We automatically collect information about your use of our Services and your device, including your IP Address, device model, browser version, operating system type, operating system version, mobile network information, device settings, and software data.
  • Location Information. We derive information about your general location (city, country) from your IP Address.  StorLab does not collect precise location data, such as GPS coordinates, that could be used to pinpoint your physical location.
  • Information from Others. We collect information about you when we receive it from other users, third parties, and affiliates. For example, when you connect your account to third-party services or sign in using a third-party partner (like Google Play Game Services, iOS Game Center or Facebook), we collect information in accordance with the privacy policies and authorization procedures determined by those services.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect to deliver, personalize, and improve our Services. We also use the information we collect to:

  • Personalizethe Services and provide advertisements, content, or features that are targeted toward your interests;
  • Save and associateyour activity across our Services and your different devices as well as associate any accounts you may use across StorLab Services together. We may associate activity and accounts under a single user ID;
  • Carry out or support promotions;
  • Detect and defendagainst fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful activity

Sharing of Information

We share the information we collect within the StorLab family of brands. We do NOT sell, license or share information that individually identifies our users with companies, organizations or individuals outside of StorLab unless one of the following circumstances applies:

  • With Your Consent.We will share information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of StorLab when we have your consent to do so.
  • With Service Providers.We provide user information to trusted partners who work on our behalf based on our instructions and in compliance with appropriate confidentiality measures.

Your Choices

  • Accounts. In most cases, you can easily delete your data at any time by deleting the app and/or resetting your Advertiser ID. We can no longer associate any stored data with you. If you have linked a social account (e.g. FaceBook, Google++ or GameCenter), then you can unlink the app with those social providers to disassociate yourself with any stored data. If you do this, your unique username and gameplay may no longer be retrievable. If you have any further questions regarding remove of data, please contact storlab.info@gmail.com.
  • Cookies. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. If you prefer, you can usually choose to set your browser to remove or reject cookies; however, the Services may not function properly if you do so.
  • Notifications. You have the ability to turn application notifications on and off by adjusting the settings of your mobile device. If you wish to allow or discontinue receiving Push Notifications or Local Notification, please consult the settings on your mobile device.

Information Security

StorLab has adopted commercially reasonable technical, administrative and physical safeguards in place to help protect against unauthorized access, use or disclosure of customer information we collect or store.

Data Retention

We store the information we collect about you for as long as is necessary for the purpose(s) for which we originally collected it. We may retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or as required by law.

Protecting Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not intended for use by children. We do not knowingly collect, use, or share information from children under age 16. If you believe that your child may have provided us with personal information, please contact us at storlab.info@gmail.com.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

StorLab may change this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make a change, we will notify you by revising the “Effective Date” date above and, in some cases, we may provide you with more prominent notice. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy frequently to stay informed about our information practices and your privacy choices.
